Why is JAY-Z one of the biggest influences in hip-hop? JAY-Z has been the driving force of hip-hop since he first started, with his unique blending of lyricism, complex themes, and excellent production skills. He brought a new meaning to rap, showing how there can be great meaning behind the words. He is often seen as one of the best rappers, not only because of his great lyrics and albums, but also because of his ability to remain relevant and produce fresh music as he ages, which many rappers were unable to keep up with.
